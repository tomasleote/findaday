# PRP-11: Off-Page SEO & Backlink Strategy

## Title
Off-Page SEO & Backlink Strategy

## Priority
Long-Term

## Objective
Accelerate Domain Authority (DA) growth through active backlink acquisition, external validation, and PR campaigns.

## Problem Statement
Technical SEO and content establish relevance, but Google needs off-page authority signals (links from reputable domains) to grant top-tier rankings for competitive keywords like "vacation planner."

## Scope
- Organize a Product Hunt launch strategy.
- Develop a "Link Magnet" asset (e.g., a free downloadable Group Trip Planning Template or survey statistics on group coordination burnout).
- Outreach to productivity blogs and travel publications for "Best Tools" roundups.
- Guest posting strategy.

## Out of Scope
- Paying for gray-hat or black-hat link farms (strictly forbidden).

## Technical/Operational Implementation Plan
- **Engineering**: Support the build of any visual assets, interactive web calculators (e.g., "Time Saved Calculator"), or iframe-embeddable widgets if requested.
- **Operations**:
  - Compile media lists of authors who write about Doodle, When2Meet, and productivity.
  - Draft pitches offering Find A Day as the modern alternative.
  - Submit the tool to saas aggregators (BetaList, AlternativeTo, etc.).

## SEO Impact
Increases the fundamental strength of the entire domain. A few high-DR (Domain Rating) links will cause all existing content (blogs, landing pages) to float higher in SERPs.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Product Hunt launch executed.
- Minimum of 10 new high-quality referring domains obtained within a quarter.
- At least 3 mentions in existing "Best Scheduling Apps" articles.

## Risks
- Manual outreach has a low response rate. Requires persistent, non-spammy execution to protect brand reputation.

## Estimated Effort
High (Operational, not technical)
